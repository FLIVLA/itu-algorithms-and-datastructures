# Useful Math 1 (numbers, exponents, logarithms)

1. Express $2 \cdot 2^2$ as a power of $2$

$$2\cdot 2^2 = 2^1 \cdot 2^2 = 2^{1+2}=2^3$$

2. For $b > 1$ and $N,M > 0$, which is true?

* $log_b(NM)=(log_bN)+(log_bM)$

* $log_b(N+M)=(log_bN)(log_bM)$

* $log_b(NM)=(log_bN)(log_bM)$

* $log_b(N+M)=(log_bN)+(log_bM)$
